「Misc」

In addition to my academic interest in AI, I am also fascinated by the following topics. Feel free to contact me if there is a chance for communication. I'm glad to know that we share common interests.

Cutting-edge technology Before delving into AI research, I gained experience in other fields such as human-computer interface and blockchain. I am convinced that these technologies, just like the presence of ChatGPT, will have a profound impact on our lives.
Entrepreneurship I have interviewed several successful Chinese entrepreneurs including Jingfan Wang (Forbes China 30 Under 30 2020), Yi Fang (CEO of Merit Interactive Co., Ltd.), as well as other alumni of the Intensive Training Program (ITP). Their captivating stories have led me to ponder over the following questions: What sets apart individuals who become exceptional entrepreneurs from others? How does entrepreneurship evolve and thrive on a macroscopic or microscopic scale over time?
Social Good I have participated in numerous volunteer activities aimed at supporting vulnerable individuals like assisting individuals with disabilities and teaching underprivileged children. I was astonished by the hardships they endured and always feel a sense of obligation to help them whenever I am capable.
